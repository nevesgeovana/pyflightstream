## 2. Delivered

1. PHY-05, rigid unsteady periodic propeller: the BladeSpec generic
   blade under PERIODIC 6 with rotary motion and 54 steps of 10 deg,
   the exact command flow the legacy-case reproduction proved, lean
   (no monitors). Metrics: final-step Total CL, CDi, CDo, CMy. The
   first measurement came out bit-identical to the shareable case
   9002 baseline (CL -0.0001012, CDi -0.0451749, CDo 0.0007011, CMy
   0.0286429), proving both that the promotion is faithful and that
   the 1440 monitors of the workspace case do not perturb the flow.
2. PHY-06, steady versus unsteady equivalence on the PHY-01 wing:
   the same static NACA 0012 AR-8 wing solved steady and then time
   marched for 120 steps of 0.01 s (36 chord passages at 30 m/s).
   Physical anchor: the march must asymptote to the steady solution.
   Measured: steady CL 0.337 (reproduces PHY-02's full baseline) and
   CDi 0.0049 (reproduces PHY-01), unsteady CL 0.3392 and CDi 0.0046;
   deltas +0.0022 and -0.0003, inside the declared equivalence bands
   with no recalibration needed.
3. Version-gated case registry: PhysicsCase gains ``versions`` and
   ``supports``; the default case set filters by evidence support and
   explicitly requesting an unsupported version is refused with the
   evidence reason. Both new cases are 26.120-only until the motion,
   coordinate-system, unsteady, and advanced-settings chapters are

4. References seeded through update-reference with recorded reasons;
   banded validation run landed 8 pass, 0 warn, 0 fail

   tests (script pins for both cases, the steady/unsteady script
